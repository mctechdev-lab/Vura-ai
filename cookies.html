Section 01
What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website or use a web application. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide site owners with analytics information.

In addition to traditional cookies, we may also use similar technologies including:

  • Local Storage & Session Storage: Browser-based storage used to save your session state, study progress, and settings without expiry (local storage) or only for the duration of your browser session (session storage).
  • Pixels & Web Beacons: Tiny transparent images embedded in pages or emails that allow us to understand whether content has been viewed or emails have been opened.
  • Firebase SDK Tokens: Authentication and session tokens generated by Google Firebase to securely identify your logged-in session across the Platform.

Cookies do not contain viruses and cannot access other information on your device. Most cookies are completely safe and essential for the Platform to function correctly.

Section 02
Types of Cookies We Use

We categorise cookies into four types based on their purpose. Essential cookies are always active; all other types require your consent:

Essential
Always On

These cookies are strictly necessary for the Platform to function. They enable core features like secure login, session management, account authentication, and navigation. The Platform cannot operate without them. No consent is required for these cookies.

Functional
Optional

These cookies remember your preferences to enhance your experience — such as your selected exam, preferred language, theme settings, and study notifications. Without these, you may need to re-enter preferences each visit.

Analytics
Optional

These cookies help us understand how students use Vura AI — which features are popular, where users drop off, and how we can improve the Platform. Data is collected anonymously and in aggregate. We use Google Analytics for this purpose.

Marketing
Optional

These cookies may be used to show you relevant Vura AI promotions across other websites and platforms. We do not use aggressive advertising tracking, and we never sell your browsing data to advertisers.

Section 04
Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on Vura AI are set by trusted third-party services we use to operate the Platform. We do not control these cookies directly — please review each provider's cookie policy for full details:

  • Google Firebase: Sets authentication and session tokens essential for secure login. Firebase's use of data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics (GA4): Sets analytics cookies (_ga, _gid) to provide anonymous, aggregate usage reports. All data is anonymised — no personal data is passed to Google Analytics. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Paystack: May set session cookies on their secure payment pages during checkout. Paystack does not set cookies on our main pages. See Paystack Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe: Sets fraud-prevention and session cookies on their secure payment frames. See Stripe Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts & Font Awesome CDN: These services load font and icon assets and may log your IP address and browser user agent. No tracking cookies are set by these services.

We regularly audit our third-party integrations to ensure they meet our privacy standards. We will update this list whenever we add or remove third-party services.

Section 05
Your Cookie Consent

In compliance with the EU ePrivacy Directive, GDPR, and applicable local cookie regulations, we request your consent before placing any non-essential cookies on your device. When you first visit Vura AI, you will see a cookie consent banner similar to the preview below:

Cookie Consent Banner — Preview
We use cookies to improve your study experience
Vura AI uses essential cookies to operate, and optional cookies for analytics and personalisation. You can accept all cookies, decline optional cookies, or customise your preferences at any time.

Your consent choices are:

  • Accept All: You consent to essential, functional, analytics, and marketing cookies.
  • Essential Only: Only strictly necessary cookies are placed. Analytics and marketing cookies are blocked.
  • Cookie Settings: Opens a detailed preference panel where you can enable or disable each cookie category individually.

Your consent is stored for 12 months. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the cookie settings icon in the Platform footer.

Section 06
How to Manage & Delete Cookies

You have full control over cookies on your device. Beyond our in-platform consent manager, you can also control cookies directly through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Apple Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Privacy, Search, and Services → Clear Browsing Data
  • Mobile Browsers: Access your browser's settings menu and navigate to Privacy or Site Settings to manage cookies on mobile devices.

Blocking essential cookies will prevent you from logging in and using core features of Vura AI. We recommend only blocking analytics and marketing cookies if you have privacy concerns, while keeping essential cookies enabled.

You may also opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Section 07
Cookies & Children

We treat users under the age of 18 with additional care when it comes to cookie usage:

  • We do not use advertising or behavioural profiling cookies for users we identify as being under 18 years of age.
  • Analytics data collected from younger users is aggregated and anonymised — it cannot be used to identify any individual student.
  • For users under 13 (who require parental consent to use the Platform), only essential session cookies are placed until parental consent has been verified.
  • Parents and guardians can request a full review of cookies placed during their child's sessions by contacting support@vura-ai.com.
