Section 01
Who We Are — Data Controller

  • Company: Michael Chibuzor Technology
  • Website: www.vura-ai.com
  • Data Protection Contact: support@vura-ai.com
  • Country of Registration: Federal Republic of Nigeria
Section 02
What Data We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide and improve our educational services. Here is a full breakdown of the categories of data we collect:

Data Category Specific Data Points Collection Method
Identity Data Full name, username, profile photo (optional) Registration form
Contact Data Email address, phone number (optional) Registration / profile settings
Location Data Country of residence, time zone Registration form

Section 03
How We Use Your Data

We use your data to deliver, personalise, and improve the Vura AI experience. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Create and manage your Vura AI account and verify your identity.
  • Personalise your study experience — recommending relevant exams, subjects, and question types based on your goals and performance.
  • Power the AI Tutor to provide contextually relevant explanations and study support.
  • Track and display your VUX coin balance, earning history, and study streak rewards.
  • Process subscription payments and send payment confirmations and receipts.
  • Send important account notifications, security alerts, and service updates.
  • Send promotional emails and platform news (only with your consent; you can unsubscribe anytime).
  • Analyse aggregate, anonymised usage patterns to improve Platform features, performance, and content quality.
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activities, abuse, and security threats.
  • Comply with our legal obligations under Nigerian and applicable international law.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Section 04
Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and other GDPR-applicable jurisdictions, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing your registration information, payment data, and account activity is necessary to fulfil our contract with you — i.e., to provide the Vura AI service.
  • Legitimate Interests: We process usage analytics and security data under our legitimate interest in improving the Platform and preventing fraud, where these interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Consent: We send marketing emails and use non-essential cookies only with your explicit consent, which you can withdraw at any time.
  • Legal Obligation: We may process data where required to comply with applicable laws, court orders, or regulatory requirements.
Section 05
Data Storage & Firebase (Google Cloud)

Your Vura AI data is stored securely using Google Firebase, a cloud platform operated by Google LLC. Firebase provides industry-standard security including data encryption at rest and in transit (TLS/SSL), automatic backups, and high availability infrastructure.

Your data is stored on Google Cloud servers and may be hosted in data centres located outside your country of residence (including the United States and Europe). Firebase complies with GDPR, ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, and other international data security standards.

For full details on Firebase's data handling, please refer to Google Firebase Privacy and Security.

Section 06
Third-Party Services We Use

Vura AI uses the following trusted third-party services to operate the Platform. Each service has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase: Database, authentication, hosting, and cloud functions. Privacy Policy
  • Paystack: Nigerian and African payment processing. Card data is handled by Paystack and never stored on our servers. Privacy Policy
  • Stripe: International payment processing. Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to understand how students use the Platform. Data is anonymised and aggregated.
  • Font Awesome (CDN): Icon assets loaded from the Font Awesome content delivery network. No personal data is collected.
  • Google Fonts: Typography loaded from Google's servers. Google may collect IP addresses. Google Privacy Policy

We enter into data processing agreements with all third-party processors to ensure your data is handled responsibly and in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

Section 07
Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting the privacy of young users. Our Platform is designed for exam-preparation students and we recognise that many of our users may be minors:

  • Under 13: We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If we discover such data has been collected, it will be deleted immediately.
  • Ages 13–17: Users between 13 and 17 must have parental or guardian consent to use the Platform. Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's data by contacting us.
  • Data Minimisation: We collect only the data strictly necessary for educational service delivery for younger users.
  • No Profiling for Ads: We do not use the data of users under 18 for advertising profiling or behavioural targeting.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has created an account, please contact us immediately at support@vura-ai.com. We will investigate and remove the account within 72 hours.

Section 08
Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We only share your data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party processors (Firebase, Paystack, Stripe) who assist us in operating the Platform under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: Where disclosure is required by law, court order, regulatory authority, or to protect the rights and safety of our users or the public.
  • Business Transfers: If Vura AI is acquired, merged, or undergoes a business restructure, your data may transfer to the new entity, subject to the same privacy protections.
  • Aggregated Analytics: We may share anonymised, non-identifiable aggregate statistics (e.g., "students in Nigeria average X quiz score") with partners or the public.
  • With Your Consent: Any other sharing requires your explicit, prior consent.
Section 09
Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or a jurisdiction with equivalent data rights, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right of Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").

Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.

Right to Restriction

Request we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.

Right to Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Withdraw Consent

Withdraw marketing consent at any time without affecting prior processing.

Right to Complain

Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@vura-ai.com with the subject line "Data Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days. Most requests can be fulfilled through your account settings page.

Section 10
Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Active Account Data: Retained for as long as your account remains active.
  • Deleted Accounts: Personal data is deleted or anonymised within 90 days of account deletion, except where we are legally required to retain records.
  • Payment Records: Transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by Nigerian financial regulations.
  • Security Logs: IP addresses and security logs are retained for up to 12 months to protect against fraud and abuse.
  • Support Communications: Support tickets and correspondence are retained for 2 years for quality assurance purposes.
Section 11
International Data Transfers

As a global platform, your data may be transferred to and stored in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States (where Google Firebase servers are located). We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws by relying on:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission for transfers to the US and other third countries.
  • Adequacy decisions where they exist between your country and the destination country.
  • Certified processors such as Google, Paystack, and Stripe who maintain appropriate international data transfer mechanisms.
Section 12
Security Measures

We take data security seriously and implement the following measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL. Data at rest in Firebase is encrypted by default.
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access controls ensure only authorised team members can access user data, on a need-to-know basis.
  • Secure Authentication: We support strong password requirements and encourage the use of two-factor authentication.
  • Regular Security Reviews: We conduct periodic security assessments and vulnerability checks of our systems.
  • Incident Response: In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

No method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Section 13
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Send an email notification to all registered users at least 14 days before changes take effect.
  • Display an in-platform notice prompting you to review the updated policy.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of Vura AI after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
