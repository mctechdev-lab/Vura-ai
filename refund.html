Section 01
Overview of Our Refund Policy

At Vura AI, we want every student to feel confident when subscribing to a paid plan. We understand that committing to a new platform requires trust, and we honour that trust with a straightforward, fair refund policy.

This policy applies to all paid subscription plans on the Vura AI Platform: Pro, Elite, and Ultra. It does not apply to the Free plan (which has no charge) or to VUX coins (which are a virtual reward currency with no monetary value).

Our core commitment: If you subscribe to any paid plan and request a refund within 7 calendar days of your initial payment, we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

All refunds are processed through the original payment method used at the time of subscription — either Paystack (Nigerian/African users) or Stripe (international users). Processing times may vary depending on your bank or card provider.

Section 03
Refund Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for a refund under our 7-day money-back guarantee, all of the following must be true:

  • Your refund request is submitted within 7 calendar days of your initial payment for that subscription period.
  • This is your first refund request on your Vura AI account. Repeat refund requests on re-subscriptions are not automatically eligible.
  • Your account has not been suspended or terminated for violations of our Terms & Conditions or Acceptable Use Policy.
  • You contact us through the official refund request channel — email to support@vura-ai.com — rather than initiating a chargeback directly with your bank (see Section 9).

We have a "no questions asked" policy for refunds within 7 days. You do not need to explain why you want a refund — simply contact us and we will process it promptly.

In addition to the 7-day window, we will also issue refunds in the following circumstances regardless of timing:

  • Double billing: If you were charged twice for the same subscription period due to a payment processing error.
  • Wrong plan charged: If you were billed for a plan tier different from the one you selected.
  • Platform failure: If a critical feature of your paid plan was completely unavailable for more than 72 consecutive hours due to a fault on our side (not external factors or scheduled maintenance).
Section 04
Non-Refundable Situations

The following situations are not eligible for a refund:

  • After 7 days: Refund requests submitted after the 7-day window from initial payment, for reasons other than billing errors or platform failure.
  • Renewal charges: Automatic monthly renewal charges are non-refundable. Cancel your subscription before the renewal date to avoid being charged for the next period.
  • Downgrade requests: Refunds are not issued for the price difference when downgrading from a higher to a lower plan mid-cycle. The downgrade takes effect at your next billing date.
  • Poor exam results: We sincerely hope every Vura AI student passes their exam, but we do not offer refunds based on exam outcomes, scores, or results, as these depend on many factors outside our control.
  • Feature dissatisfaction after 7 days: If you have used the Platform for more than 7 days and are dissatisfied with a specific feature, we encourage you to contact support so we can assist, but a refund is not automatically applicable.
  • Accounts terminated for violations: If your account was terminated due to a breach of our Terms & Conditions or Acceptable Use Policy, subscription fees are forfeited and not eligible for refund.
  • VUX coins and virtual rewards: VUX coins, reward unlocks, and other virtual currency items have no monetary value and are entirely non-refundable.
  • Repeat refund abuse: Users who subscribe and request refunds repeatedly (more than once per 12-month period) may be denied future refund requests at our discretion.

If you are unsure whether your subscription renewed, you can check your billing history in your Vura AI account settings at any time. We also send email receipts for every successful charge.

How to Request a Refund

Requesting a refund is quick and straightforward. Follow these steps:

01
Email Our Support Team
Send an email to support@vura-ai.com with the subject line "Refund Request". Please include your full name, the email address registered to your Vura AI account, and your subscription plan (Pro, Elite, or Ultra).
02
Include Your Payment Reference
If possible, include your payment confirmation email or transaction reference number from Paystack or Stripe. This helps us locate your payment quickly and process your refund faster.
03
Receive Confirmation Within 24 Hours
Our support team will acknowledge your refund request within 24 business hours and confirm whether your request is eligible. If approved, we will initiate the refund immediately.
04
Receive Your Refund
Once approved, the refund will be returned to your original payment method. Your account will revert to the Free plan. You can continue using the Free plan indefinitely.

You do not need to give a reason for your refund request within the 7-day window — we trust you. Simply send the email and we will take care of the rest.

Section 06
Refund Processing Timeline

Once a refund is approved by our team, the timeline for funds to appear in your account depends on your payment method and financial institution:

  • Paystack (Nigerian bank cards / bank transfers): Refunds typically appear within 3 to 7 business days, depending on your bank's processing time.
  • Stripe (international credit/debit cards): Refunds typically appear within 5 to 10 business days. Some international banks and card networks may take up to 15 business days.
  • Confirmation email: You will receive an email confirmation from us and from Paystack/Stripe as soon as the refund is initiated on our end.

If you do not see your refund after 10 business days from our confirmation email, please contact your bank or card issuer first, as processing delays are typically on their end. If the issue persists, contact us at support@vura-ai.com.

Vura AI does not charge any processing fees for refunds. The full subscription amount paid will be returned to you.

Section 07
VUX Coins & Refunds

VUX coins are a virtual reward currency earned through platform activity. They are not purchased directly with real money and have no monetary value. As a result:

  • VUX coins are non-refundable under any circumstances, as they carry no real-world monetary value.
  • When a subscription refund is processed, VUX coins earned through subscription-tier bonuses (e.g., double-earn mode on Elite) during the refund period will be recalculated and adjusted to Free plan earn rates.
  • VUX coins earned through normal study activity (quizzes, games, daily logins) during the refund period are yours to keep, regardless of a subscription refund.
  • Premium study packs, badges, or features unlocked using VUX coins are not refundable in cash. If your subscription is refunded and you no longer have access to the plan tier that unlocked those items, the unlocked content may be removed from your account.
Section 08
Subscription Cancellation

Cancelling your subscription is different from requesting a refund. Here is what happens when you cancel:

  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You will continue to have access to all paid plan features until that date, after which your account automatically downgrades to the Free plan.
  • Cancellation does not trigger a refund for the remaining unused days in the current billing cycle unless your request is also within the 7-day refund window.
  • To cancel, go to Account Settings → Subscription → Cancel Plan in your Vura AI dashboard, or email support@vura-ai.com.
  • You can resubscribe at any time after cancellation. However, re-subscription is treated as a new subscription — the 7-day refund window does not reset for resubscriptions on the same account.

To avoid being charged for the next month, cancel your subscription at least 24 hours before your next billing date. You can see your next billing date in Account Settings.

Section 09
Disputed Charges & Chargebacks

We strongly encourage you to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback with your bank. Chargebacks are costly for small businesses and may result in the permanent suspension of your Vura AI account.

If you believe a charge from Vura AI on your statement is unauthorised or incorrect, please follow these steps in order:

  • Step 1 — Contact us first: Email support@vura-ai.com with details of the disputed charge. We will investigate and resolve it within 3 business days.
  • Step 2 — Give us time to respond: Please allow 3 business days for us to investigate before escalating to your bank or card issuer.
  • Step 3 — Bank dispute as a last resort: If we are unable to resolve your issue and you genuinely believe the charge is fraudulent or erroneous, you may then dispute it with your bank.

Accounts associated with fraudulent chargeback claims — where a refund has already been granted by us, or where the service was legitimately used — will be permanently suspended and may be referred to Paystack/Stripe's fraud prevention teams.
